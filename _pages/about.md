# Peiyu Tong (童培宇)

I am a sophomore undergraduate student at the [School of Data Science (SDS), Fudan University](https://sds.fudan.edu.cn/), majoring in **Data Science**.

Currently, I am focused on the theoretical analysis and algorithmic implementation of optimization techniques, aiming to bridge the gap between abstract mathematical theory and large-scale computational efficiency.

## Research Interests

* **Optimization Theory**: Specifically interested in **Second-order Methods** (Newton-type, Hessian-free optimization) and their convergence properties in non-convex settings.
* **Online Learning**: Regret analysis and adaptive strategies for sequential decision-making.
* **Numerical Algorithms**: High-performance implementations for matrix computations and large-scale optimization.
